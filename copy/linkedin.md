## About

I design and manufacture custom electronic enclosures using additive manufacturing.

If you need a housing for electronics — a PLC controller, a custom monitor, an IoT device, a retrofit for existing equipment — I handle the full process: CAD design in Fusion 360, material selection, DFM, and production on a multi-printer Bambu Lab farm.

Engineering-grade materials (ABS, ASA, PC, PA-CF). Short runs from 1 to 500 units. No tooling costs. Prototypes in days.

Based in central New Jersey, serving clients locally and nationwide.
