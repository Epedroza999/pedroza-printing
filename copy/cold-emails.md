## Tips

- Send 10–15 per batch when you have time. Don't force a schedule.
- Find contacts on LinkedIn: search "[COMPANY NAME] engineer" or "plant manager."
- Personalize the first line with something specific to their company or industry.
- Follow up once after 5–7 days if no response. Then move on.
- Track who you email in a simple spreadsheet (name, company, date, response).
