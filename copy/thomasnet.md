# Thomasnet Listing — Pedroza Printing

## Company Description

Pedroza Printing is a custom electronic enclosure manufacturer based in central New Jersey, specializing in additive manufacturing for short-run production. We design and produce engineering-grade housings for electronics, industrial controls, IoT devices, and instrumentation — from concept to finished part.

Unlike print-on-demand services, we provide in-house CAD design and DFM consultation. Clients bring a problem or rough concept, and we handle material selection, modeling, and manufacturing. Our multi-printer production farm runs engineering filaments including ABS, ASA, PC, PETG, and carbon fiber nylon, delivering functional parts with tight tolerances — not prototyping-grade plastic.

We serve manufacturers, R&D teams, and product companies who need custom enclosures without the cost and lead time of injection molding or sheet metal fabrication. Quantities from 1 to 500 units. Based in central New Jersey, serving clients locally and nationwide.

## Products & Services
