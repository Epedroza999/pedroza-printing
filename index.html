Capabilities

Design

Full CAD design in Fusion 360. DFM review included. Bring a rough concept, a napkin sketch, or existing PCB/component specs — I'll handle the rest.

Manufacturing

Multi-printer Bambu Lab production farm. FDM in engineering-grade filaments. Tight tolerances, fit checks, and functional prototypes that work like production parts.

Delivery

Short-run production from 1 to 500 units. Fast turnaround, rapid iteration. No tooling costs, no MOQ. Design changes don't cost you a new mold.

Materials

ABS

Indoor enclosures. Tough, heat-resistant, acetone-smoothable for a finished surface.

ASA

Outdoor and UV-exposed enclosures. Weather-resistant with excellent long-term stability.

PETG

General purpose. Chemical resistant, strong, and easy to produce consistently.

Polycarbonate (PC)

High-impact, high-temperature applications. Extremely tough for demanding environments.

PA-CF (Carbon Fiber Nylon)

Stiff, lightweight, professional finish. Ideal for structural enclosures and fixtures.

PLA

Prototyping and low-stress applications. Fast to produce, great for form checks and mockups.

Not sure which material is right? I'll help you pick the best option for your application and environment.
